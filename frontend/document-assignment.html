How It Works

What happens after you assign.

  • Selected employees receive an email notification with a direct link to the document.
  • The document appears in their Pending Acknowledgements list on their dashboard.
  • Employee reads the document and clicks Confirm Read.
  • Admin can track completion status in the tracker below.
  • Overdue employees receive an automatic reminder email.
  • All acknowledgements are captured in the audit trail.

Audit Note

Compliance traceability.

  • Assignment logged with: assigned by, date, document ID, version.
  • Each employee confirmation logged with: name, timestamp, IP address.
  • Records are immutable — cannot be deleted.
